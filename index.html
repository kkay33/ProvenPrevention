About Proven Prevention

Proven Prevention condenses research on the prevention of common diseases down to a short and digestable list of helpful lifestyle behaviors.

Research Backed

All advice provided is sourced from high quality scientific studies.

The research behind each suggested lifestyle behavior is summarized and available for user review. Direct links to the research are provided for those who are interesed in learning more.

Philosophy

The goal of Proven Prevention is to provide easily consumed tips for preventative lifestyle medicine. There is no need to read lengthy articles or studies in order to get reliable advice.

All advice is free to the public with the hope of spreading prevention awareness and improving community health.
